Pattern 03 Engagement Flow

Exploration

Let readers steer the story by filtering, sorting, and drilling into what matters to them specifically.

Why it works

Data is rarely one-size-fits-all. Different readers care about different slices, their country, their age group, their industry. Handing them the controls converts passive viewers into active investigators, which dramatically increases engagement and time-on-story.

How to use it

Add a filter bar, segmented control, or dropdown that rewrites the chart in place. Use FLIP transitions, record positions before and after the sort, then animate the difference, so readers can track individual data points as they move. Keep the interaction immediate: <100ms response, no loading states.
