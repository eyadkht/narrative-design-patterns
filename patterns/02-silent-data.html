Silent Data

Deliberately withhold one data point to build tension, then reveal it for maximum impact.

Why it works

Absence speaks. A barely-visible bar that turns out to be 300× larger reframes everything the reader assumed about the chart. The reveal moment hits harder because the reader built a false mental model first.

How to use it

Show the chart with the silent element ghosted or missing entirely. Let the reader sit with the incomplete picture. Then reveal it, with an animated entrance and an annotation that spells out exactly what they're seeing.

Live demo

US median annual salaries across five professions. One bar is missing. Click it, or hit the button, when you're ready to see the full picture.

Real-world example

The Guardian, The Counted individual deaths were shown as dots that accumulated silently over time. The weight of the number only landed when the full year was finally shown, making the total feel earned rather than stated.
