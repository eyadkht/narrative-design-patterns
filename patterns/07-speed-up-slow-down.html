Pattern 07 Engagement Flow

Speed-up / Slow-down

Control playback tempo so that dense, critical moments get the attention they deserve.

Why it works

Uniform animation speed treats all moments as equal, which they never are. Slowing down at volatile or complex sections is a non-verbal signal: pay attention here. Racing through the quiet parts respects the reader's time.

How to use it

Pair a manual rate slider (0.25×–4×) with auto-slow logic that detects dense or high-variance regions and cuts speed automatically. Let the reader override, but default to the version that tells the story best.

Live demo
