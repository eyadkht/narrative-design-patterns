Pattern 01 Argumentation Flow Emotion

Repetition

Repeat the same visual motif across multiple views to make a pattern feel inescapable.

Why it works

One chart can be dismissed as an anomaly. The same finding surfacing across 9, 12, or 255 small multiples is impossible to explain away, repetition builds conviction in a way that no single visualisation can.

How to use it

Arrange small multiples sharing the same time axis. Add a toggle that highlights the same year, event, or threshold across every chart at once. The simultaneous flash of yellow across the grid is the argument.

Live demo

Nine economic metrics, 2005–2022. Pick a year below and watch the same moment light up across every chart, you can't argue with a pattern that shows up everywhere at once.

Real-world example

Bloomberg, Every Mass Shooting in America the same spike, the same visual beat, repeated across years, states, and weapon types. By the time you reach the tenth chart, the conclusion feels unavoidable.
